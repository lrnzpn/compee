Compee Cares

Our Compee Cares is our very own paid extended warranty that we offer!

How does CompeeCares Work?

How does CompeeCares work? Compee has a system in place to facilitate the diagnosis and repair of any Compee purchased device. If you sense an issue with your Compee-purchased device, you can contact our CompeeConcierge or send a direct email to compee.ph@gmail.com . From there Compee will coordinate with our service provider to aid with the diagnosis and if the device needs repair, it will be sent to our service provider for repair. The cost of repair will be covered by Compee, only if the customer has paid for CompeeCares for the specific device

What exactly does CompeeCares cover?

Coverage includes almost any kind of hardware-related issues that may happen to the device?

What if my device falls accidentally and there is damage to it, is that covered under CompeeCares?

We know accidents happen, so CompeeCares also covers non-factory issues, so long as they are unintentional accidents and issues that are not caused by modifications discouraged by the manufacturer

What about the supplier-issued or manufacturer-issued warranty?

The supplier warranty and/or manufacturer warranty will still apply to the item. If in the diagnosis stage we determine that it is covered by the supplier or manufacturer warranty, we will assist in getting a repair or replacement from the supplier or manufacturer, but if it is an issue beyond the scope of the warranty, that’s what CompeeCares can cover.

How many times can I use CompeeCares?

There is no maximum amount of repairs if the device is covered by CompeeCares, but we do hope that you’re taking ample care of your device.

What about if the device needs to be completely replaced?

If the service provider determines that there is no choice but to completely replace the device, the customer will be given the option to purchase the same device or the closest similar one available at the time, at no markup. In the event of theft however, the CompeeCares discount for replacement items will still be applied, but the customer must pay for CompeeCares for the new device again if the customer wants the new device covered.

Disclaimer: Compee reserves the right to refuse to replace parts or entire devices if the team suspects fraud or misrepresentation of any kind
